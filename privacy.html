Aurum Desk Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Aurum Task Desk (“Aurum Desk”, “we”, “us”) handles information when you visit this website or use our AI-assisted writing tools.

1. Who we are

Aurum Desk is a small, privacy-conscious AI writing desk. We focus on helping older professionals prepare calm, clear and factual drafts for sensitive situations such as appeals, disputes or crisis letters.

2. Information we collect

Depending on how you use the website, we may process:

  • Technical data — such as IP address, browser type, device information and basic analytics data, mainly via standard web server logs and Google Analytics.
  • Contact details — if you write to us using the contact email (for example your name, email address and the content of your message).
  • Payment-related data — when you purchase a plan, payment is handled by third-party providers (such as Stripe, Wise, PayPal, Paddle or similar). We may see basic transaction information (amount, currency, time) but not your full card number.
  • Content you provide to the AI tools — for example your own story, timeline, evidence and documents that you choose to paste or upload for drafting.

3. How we use your information

We use information for purposes such as:

  • Operating and securing the website and AI tools;
  • Generating drafts and suggestions based on the instructions you provide;
  • Handling payments and basic accounting records;
  • Replying to support or contact messages that you choose to send;
  • Analysing anonymous or aggregated usage patterns to improve the service.

4. AI models and data retention

Aurum Desk uses remote AI infrastructure to generate drafts. Our aim is to process your input in a focused, minimal way:

  • Your text is sent to the AI model solely to generate the response you request.
  • We do not intentionally use your private letters to train our own separate models.
  • Logs on the server side may contain temporary technical traces of requests for the purpose of security, abuse prevention and debugging.

We recommend that you avoid including unnecessary sensitive information (for example full ID numbers or third-party personal data) unless it is strictly needed for your case.

5. Cookies and analytics

We may use basic analytics tools, such as Google Analytics, to understand which pages are visited and from which countries. This helps us improve the website and prioritise features. You can use your browser settings or extensions to limit tracking if you prefer.

6. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with:

  • Service providers — for example hosting providers, analytics providers and payment processors, strictly to operate the service.
  • Authorities — if required by law, or to respond to valid legal requests and protect our rights or the rights of others.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights such as accessing, correcting or deleting personal information we hold about you. You can contact us using the details on the Contact page, and we will do our best to support reasonable requests, subject to legal retention requirements.

8. Data security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect the information we handle. However, no online service can guarantee perfect security. Please be careful with the devices and networks you use to access Aurum Desk.

9. Children’s privacy

Aurum Desk is intended for adults and older professionals. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the service, law or best practices. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date below. If the changes are material, we may provide additional notice on the website.

Last updated: 2 December 2025

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please write to us using the details on the Contact page.
