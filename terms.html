Aurum Task Desk — service & use terms

These terms explain how you may use Aurum Task Desk (“the desk”, “we”, “us”) and what you can reasonably expect from this writing service. By using the website or generating letters, you accept these terms.

This page is a plain-language summary for mature users. Local laws in your country may add rights and obligations that are not fully listed here. This text is not legal advice.

1. Nature of the service

Aurum Task Desk is a private AI-assisted writing desk. It helps you structure and draft letters for situations such as disputes, insurance claims, travel issues, immigration communication or family matters.

The desk is a writing and organisation tool only. It does not represent you, contact companies on your behalf, or make decisions for you. You always choose what to send, to whom, and when.

2. Not a law firm, immigration agent, or medical provider

  • The desk does not provide legal advice, immigration advice, medical advice, or any regulated professional service.
  • No lawyer–client, doctor–patient, or similar professional relationship is created by using the desk.
  • You are responsible for checking whether any draft letter is suitable for your situation and for seeking qualified human professionals when needed.

3. Who may use the desk

The desk is designed mainly for mature adults (often 40+) who handle their own affairs. By using this site, you confirm that:

  • You are at least the minimum age required to enter into contracts in your jurisdiction.
  • You will not use the desk for fraudulent, abusive, hateful, or illegal purposes.
  • You will only provide information that you are allowed to share and that does not violate the privacy or rights of others without permission.

4. Payments and refunds

Aurum Task Desk may charge per letter, per task pack, or per subscription, depending on the product you choose. Payments are processed by third-party providers (for example, online payment platforms) and are subject to their own terms.

Unless a specific refund policy is clearly stated on the purchase page, all completed letter generation fees are typically non-refundable, because computing resources and time have already been consumed.

If you believe there has been a billing error or technical fault that prevented you from receiving a usable draft, you may contact the desk team via the Contact page so we can review the situation.

5. Privacy and data handling

The desk aims to minimise drama and exposure. In general, your information is used only to generate the drafts you request and to operate the service.

Details of how data is collected, stored and protected are described in the Privacy Policy page of this site. In any conflict between this summary and the Privacy Policy, the Privacy Policy controls.

You should avoid sending highly sensitive identifiers (such as full bank card numbers or passwords) through the prompt fields. If needed, you may add those manually in your final document before sending it to any third party.

The desk may update these terms, adjust pricing, or change features from time to time, especially during early stages of the service. Updated terms will be posted on this page with a revised date.

The desk may suspend or restrict access for users who abuse the service, attempt fraud, or repeatedly ignore fair-use boundaries.

If you have questions about these terms, or feel that a draft or charge was not handled fairly, you can contact the Aurum Task Desk team through the Contact page or by email at support@aurumdesks.com.
