Pricing & Desk Fee Model

Aurum Task Desk can be used with simple monthly packages or one‑off tasks. In every case, the financial logic is clear: the client pays one number, contributors receive a net amount, and the Desk keeps the spread as its fee.

Monthly Desk Packages

Useful for clients who prefer ongoing support: letters, content, follow‑ups, and structured communication.

Starter Desk

$250 / month

For light but recurring needs.

  • Up to 5 important letters or emails
  • AI‑assisted drafts with human editing
  • Small content pieces or notes
  • Desk fee already inside the $250

Professional Desk

$480 / month

Most common tier for US/EU professionals.

  • 1 long‑form article (2,500–3,000+ words)
  • Up to 10 short‑form posts per month
  • Help with tone, structure, and messaging
  • Desk fee already inside the $480

Private Desk Tier

$950 / month

For clients who want calm but high‑touch support.

  • Priority access for urgent tasks
  • Combination of writing, scripts, and flows
  • Guidance on what to say and how to say it
  • Desk fee already inside the $950
How the Desk Fee Works in Monthly Packages

The client only sees the package price on Stripe (for example, USD 480). Behind the scenes, the Desk decides how much of that goes to contributors. The difference between the package price and their net payout is the Desk’s coordination fee.

Per‑Task Example

Not everyone needs a monthly arrangement. Some tasks can be handled one at a time.

Single Task, Clear Spread

A client asks for a 2,000‑word article and is quoted USD 450. The Desk later agrees a net payout of USD 320 with the person who writes it. The remaining USD 130 is the Desk’s fee for scoping, refining, supervising, and delivering the work.
