What this desk does — and does not — do

We sit between “random emotional appeal” and “full law firm”. This is a writing & strategy desk focused on clarity, not on hacking systems.

You get
  • • A calm, outside view on what the platform likely saw
  • • Writing that sounds like a serious business, not a panic post
  • • Clear structure: context → policies → evidence → future controls
  • • Suggestions on which documents strengthen your case
  • • Realistic expectations about outcomes and timelines
You do not get
  • • Fake documents, edited IDs or proof of address
  • • Account rental, “replacement accounts” or shadow access
  • • Someone logging in as you or asking for passwords / 2FA codes
  • • Promises of guaranteed reinstatement
  • • Legal representation in court (we are not a law firm)
