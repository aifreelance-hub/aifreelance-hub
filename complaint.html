What your letter usually includes.
  • A one-paragraph overview of the issue and who you are.
  • A short, clear timeline with dates, amounts, and references.
  • What went wrong and why it is unreasonable or unacceptable.
  • What you are asking for (refund, correction, escalation, etc.).
  • A calm closing that keeps the door open for resolution, not conflict.
