About Aurum Task Desk

Aurum Task Desk is a small, private coordination office for AI‑assisted tasks. It is designed for older professionals, business owners, and high‑intent clients who prefer calm, structured help instead of noisy public marketplaces.

What the Desk Is

Instead of being another open platform, Aurum Task Desk acts like a discreet middle office. Clients submit tasks. Freelancers (and you, behind the scenes) execute with AI tools. The Desk coordinates expectations, timelines, and communication, and keeps a transparent fee for doing so.

How It Operates

1 — Tasks come in

Clients describe what they need in simple language: letters, articles, scripts, content, or small automations.

2 — Work is executed

Freelancers use AI plus human judgement to deliver results following Desk guidelines for tone and clarity.

3 — Settlement & desk fee

Clients pay via Stripe or agreed channels. Contributors are paid a net amount. The difference is the desk fee.

Desk Values

Calm & private

No hype. No spam. Just quiet, serious collaboration.

Clarity in words

Scopes, updates, and outputs are written in plain language.

Respect for time

Built for people who balance work, health, family, and travel.

Transparent economics

Clients pay one price, contributors know their net amount, and the desk fee is the spread.
