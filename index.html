How Aurum Task Desk Runs

Think of it as a small private office between clients and freelancers. You coordinate the relationship; AI and humans handle the work.

About the Desk & the Desk Lead

Aurum Task Desk is not a mass platform. It is a small, curated desk focused on older professionals who prefer calm, private work.

The Desk is structured like a quiet back office: clients on one side, contributors on the other, and a calm coordinator in the middle. Tasks are scoped, priced, and quality-checked before anything is delivered.

Over time, the goal is simple: a few dozen long-term relationships instead of thousands of one-off transactions. Many clients are in their 50s, 60s, or 70s, and want help putting thoughts into words without feeling rushed or judged.

AI is used like a quiet assistant — helpful in drafting and structuring, but never left alone to send raw output to older clients. Every important piece still passes through human eyes.

Privacy, safety & calm boundaries

Older clients often share personal stories and sensitive details. The Desk is designed to keep those details safe and handled with respect.

Data & confidentiality

Client messages are treated as confidential working material. Drafts can be deleted on request once a task is completed. For sensitive tasks, you can work under a non-disclosure agreement (NDA).

AI usage policy

AI is used to support drafting and structure, not to blast mass messages. No raw AI output is sent directly to older clients without human review for tone and clarity.

Payments & Stripe

All payments are processed via Stripe. The Desk does not hold deposits or client funds on-site. You see a clear client price and a clear net payout, with the Desk fee as the difference.

Full details will live in the Privacy Policy and Terms of Service pages as your desk grows.

Common Questions

Is this a company or just a tool? It is structured like a small private office. You decide if you present it as a firm, a personal desk, or part of your existing business.
Do clients need to know about AI? No. Many clients just see that their letters, posts, or scripts are done clearly and on time. The Desk quietly uses AI where it helps, but you stay in control of the tone.
How does the Desk earn money? Clients pay one amount (for example, USD 480). Freelancers or contributors receive a net amount. The difference is the Desk fee, which compensates you for scoping, coordination, and quality control.
How long does a typical task take? Simple letters and small content pieces are often turned around within a few days. Larger packs and automations are scoped with clear timelines so older clients are never left wondering.
What if a client wants changes? Most tasks assume one round of small revisions as part of the scope. For bigger changes or new ideas, you can treat them as a follow-up task with a new budget.
Can we use NDAs or delete data? Yes. For sensitive stories or business details, you can work under an NDA and request that drafts be deleted from active systems after completion, except where law or accounting requires records.
