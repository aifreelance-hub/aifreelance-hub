HOW THE DESK RUNS

Think of this as a very small private office, not a big platform. Clear scopes, AI support, human oversight, and a visible desk fee model.

For clients

  • Describe your AI-related task: writing, letters, LinkedIn content, WhatsApp flows, simple automation, research notes.
  • Set a budget range and wait for a trusted freelancer to accept the task.
  • When you both agree, pay via Stripe using a clear link. The desk keeps a fee, the freelancer receives the rest.

For freelancers

  • Log in once. Your profile and activity remain in this private desk, not on a noisy public marketplace.
  • Accept only the tasks you are comfortable with, and use AI tools to move faster with quality control.
  • You are paid from the Stripe flow, minus a desk fee that compensates the office for coordination.

For the desk owner (you)

  • You decide who is invited: older US professionals, small business owners, veterans, executives.
  • You quote total prices to clients, then privately agree a net payout with freelancers. The spread is your desk fee.
  • Over time you can also add Stripe subscriptions for ongoing content and communication support.
