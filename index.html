Platform-style examples (anonymised)

A feel for the types of disputes that fit this desk. Names and details are changed, but the patterns are real.

TikTok Shop · mid-size seller
"Health gadget" flagged as medical device
What happened
  • • New product category with "relief" wording.
  • • Live stream mentions of "pain" and "fix".
  • • Multiple reports from competitors.
What we recommended
  • • Remove medical-style claims and images.
  • • Position product as lifestyle / comfort.
  • • Provide receipts & logistics details for past orders.
Result: account reinstated, product listing re-opened
PayPal · digital service provider
Rolling 180-day hold on payouts
What happened
  • • High-ticket invoices to new markets.
  • • A cluster of disputes around one campaign.
  • • Generic "user agreement breach" notice.
What we recommended
  • • Explain business model & delivery steps.
  • • Provide contract templates and service logs.
  • • Offer to limit new regions and adjust risk.
Result: partial early release + clearer risk expectations
Facebook Ads · performance agency
Business Manager repeatedly disabled
What happened
  • • Multiple clients with aggressive offers.
  • • One client mixing compliant and non-compliant verticals.
  • • Policy violations flagged across several ad accounts.
What we recommended
  • • Separate risk by client and vertical.
  • • Document compliance checks for "clean" clients.
  • • Present a new structure and internal review process.
Result: one BM reinstated, others migrated safely
