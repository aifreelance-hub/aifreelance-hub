Services · what happens on AIFreelance Hub
One marketplace, two sides: clients post AI-friendly tasks, freelancers deliver using AI tools plus real-world judgement and editing.
For clients

Post tasks for content, images, presentations, simple automations, data clean-up and more. Describe your outcome and deadline – not which AI model to use.

For AI freelancers

How AIFreelance Hub works
A simple three-step flow you can explain to anyone in a sentence or two.
1 · Define the task
  • Client describes what they need in plain language.
  • Picks a category: writing, images, data, automation.
  • Sets budget and preferred deadline.
2 · Match with AI freelancers
  • Pre-screened freelancers apply with a short proposal.
  • Client picks the best fit based on scope and price.
  • Work is delivered in 24–72 hours for most tasks.
3 · Approve & share the fee
  • Client approves final work and completes payment.
  • Freelancer receives their share (e.g. 70–85%).
  • Platform keeps a clear, visible fee.
FAQ
Short answers you can expand later into a full help center or documentation.
Do I need to be a developer?

No. Most AI freelancers here are writers, designers, marketers and operators who are simply good at using AI tools to get things done faster.

Which countries can use it?

Clients mainly come from the US, UK and EU. Freelancers can apply from most countries as long as they can receive payments via supported methods.

How does the platform make money?

Transparent fees on completed projects, plus optional “Pro” upgrades later. No hidden charges, no selling user data.
