How to use this Task Marketplace

Treat this page as your private board. You can rename tasks, adjust budgets, and decide which ones are visible when you invite clients or freelancers to your Aurum Task Desk.

For clients
  • Send a message or email describing what you need in plain words.
  • The Desk turns it into a clear task with scope and budget.
  • You approve the budget and pay via Stripe for each task.
For freelancers
  • You only see tasks from this private desk, not from the whole internet.
  • Most work is AI-assisted but human-checked for tone and clarity.
  • Payouts are net of a desk fee for coordination and quality control.

In a real setup, each task card here can later be linked to a form, Stripe Checkout, or your own onboarding process. For now, this page is a clear, consulting-style overview of the kind of work your Aurum Task Desk is built to handle.
