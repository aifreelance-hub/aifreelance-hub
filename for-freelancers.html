How you earn on AIFreelance Hub
You’re not selling “hours”. You’re selling outputs: content bundles, image packs, cleaned datasets, simple systems – with clear starting prices.
1 · Turn your skills into offers
  • Pick 2–3 services you can confidently deliver using AI tools.
  • Define a starting scope (for example “up to 5 pages”).
  • Set a starting price that feels fair for both you and the client.
2 · Apply to tasks that fit you
  • Clients post tasks that match your skills: writing, images, data and more.
  • You apply with a short, direct proposal and timeline.
  • When chosen, you deliver using AI + your own judgement.
3 · Deliver clearly and communicate often
  • Use AI to create drafts, variations and structure quickly.
  • Always do a human review and make decisions yourself.
  • Communicate: send progress updates, ask when unclear.
4 · Get paid after completion
  • After client approval, the project is marked complete.
  • AIFreelance Hub keeps a platform fee (example 20–30%).
  • You receive the remaining amount via the available payment methods.
